Inception (2010)

Synopsis: Directed by Christopher Nolan, this mind-bending thriller explores the concept of dreams within dreams, featuring a stellar cast led by Leonardo DiCaprio.

Director: Christopher Nolan

Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy

Reviews: "Inception is a visually stunning and intellectually captivating masterpiece that keeps you guessing until the very end." - IMDb

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Synopsis: Directed by Frank Darabont and based on a Stephen King novella, this drama follows the story of a banker wrongly convicted of murder and his experiences in Shawshank State Penitentiary.

Director: Frank Darabont

Cast: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman

Reviews: "The Shawshank Redemption is a timeless classic with powerful performances and a poignant story that stays with you long after the credits roll." - Rotten Tomatoes

The Godfather (1972)

Synopsis: Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this classic crime film chronicles the story of the powerful Italian-American mafia family of Don Vito Corleone, played by Marlon Brando, and his son Michael, played by Al Pacino.

Director: Francis Ford Coppola

Cast: Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, James Caan, Robert Duvall

Reviews: "The Godfather is a cinematic masterpiece, with impeccable performances, rich storytelling, and iconic moments that have solidified its place in film history." - Empire Magazine
